IoT CoE Connect Program

  • IoT (Internet of Things) refers to set of connected devices, networks and back end systems together enabling advanced services and automation.
  • Centre of Excellence refers to facility with necessary hardware, software and expertise to guide students in the Field of IoT domain
  • To bridge the gap between the skills sets to match that of the Industry level using the set up provided by RDL.
  • To encourage students to pursue R&D on the latest technologies, IoT, Li-Fi, Robotics & Automation, Wireless sensor network & Network security.
  • Enable designated faculty members with well documented training, IoT design flowcharts, checklist and assessment material
