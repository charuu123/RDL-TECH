Asset Condition Monitoring

The Challenge

  • The primary concerns of clients include minimizing downtime,
    • Maintaining the health of assets,Minimizing wear and tear, Avoiding unusual operating force that could result in quality flaws.

Benefits

  • Machine uptime increased up to 10%.
  • Condition-based maintenance reduces maintenance costs by 20%.

Asset Covered

  • CNC Machines.
  • Compressors.
  • Hydraulic Pumps.

Machine Data

  • Available Time, Run Time, Idle Time, Down Time, AUTO/MDI/JOG,
  • Running Job, Spindle Speed, Load, Alarm Time & Type, Operator Log.
  • Energy Parameters: Voltage (P-P/P-N), Amps (R, Y, B), KWh, KVA, PF.
  • Vibration (FFT & Magnitude) and Force on a real-time basis.
