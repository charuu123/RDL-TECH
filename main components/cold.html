Cold Storage Monitoring Solution

Additional Information


Equipping your cold storage facility with an IoT-enabled cold storage monitoring solution helps to improve food safety and automate compliance reporting With continuous refrigerator/freezer temperature monitoring and regular maintenance of the conditions inside the facility.The storage time for temperature-sensitive perishable goods varies from a few days to a few years.Cold storage monitoring solution ensures safety and quality as well as reduced spoilage, and improved energy efficiency.


Benefits of implementing IoT solutions for cold storage facilities

  • Real-time monitoring
  • Employee and asset safety
  • Increases profitability
  • Reduced Spoilage
  • Improved energy efficiency

Application

  • Agriculture industry
  • Blood banks
  • Food & beverage industry
  • Healthcare industry
  • Pharmaceutical facilities
  • Restaurant chains
  • Food manufacturing facilities

Cold Storage Data Logger WiFi / GPRS / 4G / LTE

Cold Storage Data Logger comes with IP 68 enclosure & supports a variety of IO interfaces like Analog , Digital , MODBUS RTU , MODBUS TCP with multiple Temperature sensor interfacing options . This Data logger can be used in Cold Storage monitoring system , Food storage & Processing system . Data Logger product is designed to seamlessly integrate with the IoT and Analytical processing systems. Supporting multiple I/O options, interfaces data logger is a perfect fit to build custom automation solutions The Device support SSL with multiple configurable protocol like JSON / MQTT / FTP.
